Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian dish made with pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup béchamel sauce
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  2. Cook the beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and spices to the meat, simmer 10–15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers and finish with cheese on top.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
