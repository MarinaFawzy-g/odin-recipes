Basbousa

Description

Basbousa is a sweet Middle Eastern dessert made from semolina and soaked in sugar syrup.

It has a soft, crumbly texture and is often topped with almonds or coconut.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup coconut
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Sugar syrup (as needed)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour mixture into greased baking dish.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden.
  5. Pour cold syrup over hot Basbousa.
