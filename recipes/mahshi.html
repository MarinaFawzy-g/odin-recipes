Mahshi 🥬

Mahshi is a traditional Egyptian dish made by stuffing vegetables like zucchini, peppers, and eggplant with a rice and herb mixture.

Ingredients 📝

  • 4 zucchinis
  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Chopped parsley, dill, coriander
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, and spices

Instructions 👩‍🍳

  1. Hollow out the vegetables.
  2. Mix rice, herbs, tomato paste, and spices.
  3. Stuff each vegetable with the mixture.
  4. Arrange in a pot and add tomato sauce or broth.
  5. Simmer for 45–60 minutes until cooked.
